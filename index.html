About Me

Hello World! My name is Skylar Krieger, I am a UX Professional from the SF Bay Area. I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from SFSU in December 2020. Over the course of my career I designed and developed B2B, B2C, and Internal products at SAP, Ericsson, and PennyMac. I have touched all parts of the product lifecycle from proof of concept and initial prototyping to customer adoption to ensuring continuous integration and deployment and everything inbetween.

Customer Problem

Wine and cheese pairing can be hard. Especially when you're unfamiliar with wine culture and working on a limited budget.

Solution

Wine Meet Cheese is a mobile app designed to help wine novices find perfect wine and cheese pairing. By scanning the barcode of either a bottle of wine or a block of cheese, this application will suggest a match. As the frontend lead, I designed the simple and intuative UI and workflow.
